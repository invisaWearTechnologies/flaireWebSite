Flaire

Flaire is a coin-sized component that fits into wearable devices. When pressed, it communicates with a connected phone to send alert messages (containing location information) to pre-configured contacts or police.


  • Instantly contact friends, family or police in an emergency
  • Flaire vibrates to let you know when help is on its way
  • Easily fits into different pieces of jewlery to maintain discreetness
  • Alarm
    Feature to enable loud noise to deter attacker
  • Up-To-Date GPS
    Allows contacts to constantly retrieve location updates
  • BLE Technology
    Allows Flaire to communicate with a connected phone within 100 m
