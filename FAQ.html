  • +What is the aim of invisaWear Technologies?

      Flaire by invisaWear Technologies aims to offer individuals the ability to contact emergency services with the touch of a button.

  • +Do I have to buy a separate component for each piece of jewelry?

      No. All you have to do is remove the Flaire component from the existing piece of jewelry and install it into another piece of jewelry purchased from our company.

  • +What are the return policies?

      In order to return your Flaire, the only requiremet is that their is no damage to the jewelry or component.
      Flaire may be returned within a span of 60 days.

  • +How do I setup the app on my phone?

      Please go to the app store and search for invisaWearTechnologies. Select the Flaire Companion app and download the
      application onto your phone.

  • +What platfroms does Flaire support?

      iOS

      Fliare will work on the following iOS devices running iOS 8 and above:

      iPhone 4S
      iPhone 5, iPhone 5C & iPhone 5S
      iPhone 6, iPhone 6S & iPhone 6 Plus

      Android

      Flaire will work on the following Android devices running Android 4.4 and above:

      Samsung Galaxy S4
      Samsung Galaxy S5
      Samsung Galaxy S6 & S6 Edge
      Nexus 5 & 6

      If your device is not on the above list, we can't guarantee that it will be compatible.
      We are working to expand the number of supported devices and will update this list frequently.

  • +Can I get Flaire Wet?

      Yes, but it can not be submerged in water. The techonology is splash-resistant so a little rain is fine,
      but swimming or showering should be avoided.

  • +How close does my phone need to be to my Flaire technology?

      Flaire uses Bluetooth Low Energy technology. Your Flaire and phone will need to be within 100 meters of each other to ensure
      you can send alerts.
