FAQ's

What is the aim of invisaWear Technologies?

Our goal is to keep you safe on the street. Flaire makes it easier to call for help during an emergency.


Do I have to buy a separate component for each piece of jewelery?

No you do not. All you have to do is remove the Flaire component from the existing piece of jewelry and install it into another piece of jewelery purchased from our company. To learn more, please visit the "How to Set Up the Device" page.


What are the return policies?

In order to return your Flaire, the only requiremet is that their is no damage to the jewelery or component. Flaire may be returned within a span of 60 days.


How do I setup the app on my phone?

Please go to the app store and search for invisaWearTechnologies. Select the Flaire Companion app and download the application onto your phone.
