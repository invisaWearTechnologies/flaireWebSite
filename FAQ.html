  • +What is the aim of invisaWear Technologies?

      Our goal is to keep you safe on the street.

  • +Do I have to buy a separate component for each piece of jewelery?

      No you do not. All you have to do is remove the Flaire component from the existing piece of jewelry
      and install it into another piece of jewelery purchased from our company. To learn more, please visit the
      "How to Set Up the Device" page.

  • +What are the return policies?

      In order to return your Flaire, the only requiremet is that their is no damage to the jewelery or component.
      Flaire may be returned within a span of 60 days.

  • +How do I setup the app on my phone?

      Please go to the app store and search for invisaWearTechnologies. Select the Flaire Companion app and download the
      application onto your phone.

  • +What platfroms does Flaire support?

      iOS:
      Fliare will work on the following iOS devices running iOS 8 and above with support for Bluetooth Low Energy:
      iPhone 4s, iPhone 5, iPhone 5S, iPhone 5C, iPhone 6, iPhone 6S and iPhone 6 Plus. Flaire does not currently support tablets.
      Android:
      Flaire works on the following Android phones running Android 4.4 and above with Bluetooth 4.0:
      Samsung Galaxy S4
      Samsung Galaxy S5
      Samsung Galaxy S6 & S6 Edge
      Nexus 5 & 6
      If your device is not on the above list but uses Android 4.4 and Bluetooth 4.0, there is a good chance Flaire will work, but we can't guarantee it at this time.
      We are working to expand the number of supported Android devices over the next few weeks and will update this list frequently.
