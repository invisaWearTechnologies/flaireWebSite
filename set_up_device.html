Please Follow the below steps to setup the device:

  • Install the Flaire Companion App from the App Store.
  • Fill out the Personal Information Page.
  • Fill out the Additional Information Page.
  • Fill out the Emergency Contact Information Page.
  • Connect Your Device to the Phone.
  • Your Device will now be ready for use! Press the button on the component in the event of an emergency.
