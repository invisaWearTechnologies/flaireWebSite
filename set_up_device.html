How to Set Up the Device

Please Follow the below steps to setup the device:

  • 1. Install the Flaire Companion App from the App Store.
  • 2. Fill out the Personal Information Page.
  • 3. Fill out the Additional Information Page.
  • 4. Fill out the Emergency Contact Information Page.
  • 5. Connect Your Device to the Phone.
  • 6. Your Device will now be ready for use! Press the button on the component in the event of an emergency.
