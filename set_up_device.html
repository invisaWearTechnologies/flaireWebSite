How to Set Up the Device

Follow the below steps to setup the device:

  • 1. Press Setup to configure the app.
  • 2. Fill out the Personal Information Page
  • 3. Fill out Additional Information Page.
  • 4. Fill out the Emergency Contact Info
  • 5. Connect Device to Phone
  • 6. Your Device will now be ready for use! Press the button in the event of an emergency.
